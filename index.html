Our Story

Building up a liveaboard diving safari boat was the life dream of three mid age men whom are Ahmed Tawik (Tifa), Amr Shatilla (ASH) and Raafat Soliman (R.S).

Since they met early 2000s a special spark of friendship took place and it started to grow with time. Over the years, they realized their intense passion towards scuba diving.

They pointed out their common vision towards the best diving destination in the world. Their belief in the extreme natural beauty of the red sea dive sites in Egypt magnified the idea of constructing a unique well-crafted liveaboard safari boat.

They believe that an outstanding liveaboard safari is an essential factor to draw memorable, exceptional diving experience and to fulfill the optimum adventure of the diver's vacation. ​

Late 2014 they decided to make the dream come true and to do whatever it takes to see their Hammerhead (HH) sailing in the red sea. And they pull oneself together, gathered and blended all their life knowledge and experience in the various fields to design their Hammerhead (HH).​v

Hammerhead Fleet

​​HH is a new luxuries fleet with two live aboard diving safari boats (Hammerhead I and II). Hammerhead I (HH I) started its operation in March 2017 while Hammerhead II (HH II) started its operation in October 2019. Both boats operate in the red sea and were perfectly designed to meet diver’s needs and requirements during a live aboard diving safari trips to ensure outstanding and unique experience.
